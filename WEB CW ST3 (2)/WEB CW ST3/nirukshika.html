Who are we?

We, the Software Engineering students from Group C(2) at the Informatics Institute of Technology, Sri Lanka, have undertaken this project with a focus on promoting and understanding the multifaceted concept of good health and well-being. This topic holds significant relevance as it directly impacts the individual and societal spheres, and we are committed to delving into it throughout the assignment

What about this?

Over the last 15 years, the number of childhood deaths has been cut in half. This proves that it is possible to win the fight against almost every disease. Still, we are spending an astonishing amount of money and resources on treating illnesses that are surprisingly easy to prevent. The new goal for worldwide Good Health promotes healthy lifestyles, preventive measures and modern, efficient healthcare for everyone.
