Who are we?

We, the Software Engineering students from Group C(2) at the Informatics Institute of Technology, Sri Lanka, have undertaken this project with a focus on promoting and understanding the multifaceted concept of good health and well-being. This topic holds significant relevance as it directly impacts the individual and societal spheres, and we are committed to delving into it throughout the assignment
